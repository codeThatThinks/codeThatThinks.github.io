Classic Computer Prank

This prank will confuse the user because he or she will think there are icons on the desktop when there are not.

Vista:

1. First you need to take a picture of your desktop. To do this, press the Prt Scrn (Print Screen) button on your keyboard. It should be just above the Insert button.

2. Run Microsoft Paint by going to Start > Programs > Accessories > Paint or typing mspaint into the run dialog box.

3. After paint has started, press the Cntrl (Control) and V keys on your keyboard at the same time. The image of your desktop should appear in Paint. Save the file in your My Documents folder as background.bmp.

4. Right-click on your desktop (not on an icon) and select Personalize from the popup menu. Under Personalize appearance and sounds, click Desktop Background. Click the Browse button and locate background.bmp. Click Ok.

5. Right-click on your desktop and go to View > Show Desktop Icons on the popup menu. The prank is complete! To undo this prank, Repeat step 5 and change your desktop background.
